Brewstone
UnityC#
View on itch.io
Brewstone was made in a team of 4 for the University of Portsmouth Game Jam 2020 where it won awards for Best Design, Best Overall and People's Choice. I took the role of programmer and 3D animator.

We tried to incorporate all three of the Jam' themes in our game: the abandoned village of Bockhampton, the microbiologist Jillian Banfield and a 1999 Canadian curling tournament. You play as a robot who brews potions in his belly from ingredients you pick up around the world, and launch those potions on curling stones to attack enemies and clear obstacles.
