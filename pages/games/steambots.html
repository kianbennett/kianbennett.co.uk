Steambots was made in a team of 4 for the Game Parade Spring 2022 Game Jam where it won awards for Most Commercially Viable, Best 3D Art and Best Level Design, and I took the role of programmer and animator. You control three malfunctioning robots trying to escape the factory in which they were created, using each robot's unique abilities together to solve puzzles.

Steambots has been showcased at events like EGX, Guildford Games Fest and Venturefest South.
