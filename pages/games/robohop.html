RoboHop
UnityC#
View on itch.io
RoboHop was made over one week in teams of four to fit the theme 'Mario platformer'. The game contains all the core Mario features (ducking, ground pound, teleporting pipes) as well as a grappling hook to swing from. The player can pick up powerups to let them run faster and jump higher to overcome certain obstacles.

Robohop was made in Unity with C# - I took the role of programmer in the group. It ranked 2nd in our year group.
