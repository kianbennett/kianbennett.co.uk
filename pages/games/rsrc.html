[rs,rc] is a mobile puzzle platformer. The player controls two connected blocks and can switch between the two of them (each colour interacts with the level differently). The player can drag one block towards the other along the line that connects them to overcome obstacles. Each level has an optional collectible for an added challenge!

[rs,rc] was made in Unity with C# and has builds for Android and PC.
