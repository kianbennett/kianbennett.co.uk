Jelly Arena
View on itch.io
Jelly Arena is a multiplayer arena brawler where the player can customise their jelly's abilities and appearance. There is a wide array of ranged and melee abilities each with their own ability skins. The player can level up their profile, unlock achievements and add their scores to the online leaderboard.

Made in Unity with C#, the multiplayer is handled with Unity's networking solution UNet.
