Minecrawlers
UnityC#ProcGen
View on itch.io View on Bitbucket
Minecrawlers is a genre-blend between minesweeper and a twin stick shooter, made for the 2022Grads In Games Rising Star programming challenge where it was the highest ranked project in every category. The enemies are procedurally generated clusters of blocks, some of which contain bombs, and player must shoot the empty blocks and disable all the bomb blocks to defeat the enemy.

As a part of the challenge I wrote a document detailing the development process and technical challenges I faced, and can be viewed here.
