Smash Bots
UnityC#
View on itch.io
Smash Bots was made over two weeks in teams of five to fit the theme 'fighting game'. Two players choose a robot (each with slightly different stats) and can jump, punch, block and shoot to win a best of three fight. The game also features fully customisable key bindings.

As the group's programmer I worked closely with the artist to ensure the animations matched the movement and correctly telegraphed the players actions (essential in a fighting game), and I believe the result is user inputs that feel satisfying and responsive.

Smash Bots was made in Unity with C# and was ranked 1st in our year group.
