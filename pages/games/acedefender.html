Ace Defender was made over three weeks in groups of six to fit the theme 'Star Fox / R-Type'. The player must control their ship to shoot oncoming enemies with varying movement/shooting patterns, and can of course do a barrel roll to deflect enemy shots. There is an intro cutscene and tutorial level explaining the game's controls led by a charming companion character.

I didn't have time to flesh out the gameplay as much as I wanted - there is only one type of attack and the enemy types are identical with different movement/shooting patterns. I'm happy with the aiming/movement system but there were issues with some enemies being outside the player's range.

Ace Defencder was made in Unity with C# - I took the role of programmer and designer in our group. It ranked 2nd in our year group.
