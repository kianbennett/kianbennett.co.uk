About Me
I'm Kian, a final year Computer Games Technology student at the University of Portsmouth, specialising in programming. I am particularly interested in creating gameplay systems but I am passionate about all areas of game development. I have been making games for years outside of school, covering a variety of genres and art styles, and I am always eager to learn new concepts.

For my placement year I took an software engineering internship at Rare, working closely with the design team to implement features for Sea of Thieves, as well as engine work improving the game's server perfomance. In 2021 and 2022 I won the Grads In Games Rising Star programming category with my games Minecrawlers and Lordship, and last spring the my team and I won awards for Most Commercially Viable, Best 3D Art and Best Level Design in the Game Parade Game Jam with our game Steambots.
